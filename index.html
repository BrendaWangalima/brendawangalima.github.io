Content & Article Writing

Great content changes attitudes, solves problems, builds businesses and moulds markets. By creating unique content, I and my clients are on a mission to build a more-informed world. This is why I champion one ideal above all else - the power of awesome content.

Blogging

Bloggers are a lot more than glorified storytellers. They bear a mighty responsibility: to create a commanding and inspiring presence that can shape the lives of their audience. When tasked with writing a blog post, I do so bearing this responsibility in mind. I give you the best.

Academic Writing

Get your academic work written in your style of choice, whether APA, MLA, Chicago or other. Familiarity with these styles ensures you get exactly what you want – academically speaking.

Copywriting

Writing copy is quite different from writing an article. Copywriting requires mastery of the ever-changing market scenarios across a wide range of online media. As a polymath, I write copy for diverse clients; from paint manufacturers to courier services, I always deliver enthralling copy.

Press Releases

It takes a couple of seconds for a reader to determine whether your Press Release has anything valuable to offer them. As lead generation and conversion tools, Press Releases should be engaging and use as few words as possible to get the message across.

Social Media Management

Social Media Engagement means interacting, capturing and holding the attention of your followers. It means getting the message across in an unforgettable manner. Leave your social media management to me and get true engagement and not just meaningless “shares” and “likes”.

Experience

Experience comprises the lessons and insights that one gets from doing something over and over again, for several years. Experience allows me to spot potential problems early and this helps my clients save time and money in their efforts to get compelling content. You can never go wrong when you hire a person with experience; I have plenty of that!

Skill

You may scratch your head and ask, “What? You mean writing is a skill?” My answer is always, “Hell, Yes!” You need skills in order to write a piece that truly resonates with an audience. The piece should be well thought-out and edited carefully. All of these are skills and it took me several years to develop them, for your current and future benefit.

Time

Time is a jealously guarded commodity for all entrepreneurs. So when you give me work I ensure that I put in ample time to do it properly. You then have time to focus on the core functions of your business, knowing that your content creation is in capable hands. Time is a valuable product, must be valued as such, and spent doing great work; create great content.

Heart

There are times when you read something and say to self, “The author’s heart wasn’t in it”. When I write for a client, I put my heart in it; I am proud of the final product and do give a damn about it. A good heart will give you a lot more than you had expected.

Thought

Before an idea is born, there must be a thought. A competent content writer strives to understand your goals, objectives and limitations before starting on your work or making any recommendations. Many people think of replication as the easiest way to make money, but uniqueness is the true goose that will lay your golden egg. I always give unique, well thought-out content to my clients.

My Awesome WorkFlow

Built over the years and it works well every single time

Research

I carefully examine what your ultimate goal is when you give me a topic to write on. I strive to understand the underlying reason, motivation and influence that the content is to be built upon. I structure the piece to related perspectives, social drivers and marketing objectives that will eventually affect the decision of the reader, so you get the intended result.

Write

I ensure that the content flow will facilitate the readers’ journey by understanding the premier questions that the content should address along the way. Great content is not just about writing an interesting story, but about addressing the needs of readers in a way that resonates with them.

Refine

A good writer will first pour out their thoughts on the page and then go back and carefully refine the piece. Grammar and spelling must be addressed. The flow must be comprehensible. I read and reread all the content I write before I deliver it to you.

Deliver

I am a stickler for strict turn-around-times; I do my best to deliver the content on time. Should there be unexpected delays, I will communicate with you before the expected deadline. I also give value added services like writing summaries on Medium.com and linking to your site (service available to first-person content owners and not middlemen).
